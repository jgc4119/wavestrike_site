About

WaveStrike is a woman-owned small business founded to provide Software Engineering services to the Department of Defense and Intelligence Community. We are a premium provider of technology solutions in the areas of Agile Software Development, Cloud Storage and Retrieval Solutions, Big Data Analytics, Web Development, Distributed Computing, and DevOps. We pride ourselves in our ability to provide innovative solutions to our customers' most challenging problems from design to deployment. Our corporate philosophy places technical excellence, motivated employees, project efficiency, and customer satisfaction as the foundation of everything we do.

Leadership

WaveStrike was founded in 2010 by a team of individuals who had worked together at various points in their long careers in the Intelligence Community. This team decided it was time to take their lessons learned and create the company that was their vision of what they had learned over the years as the ideal place to work. With backgrounds in technology, software engineering, consulting, business, and program management the leadership team of WaveStrike brings together a strong history of over 50 collective years supporting the Intelligence Community. With career histories working at both large and small businesses, the leadership team was able to create a culture that took the best aspects of each place they worked to form the WaveStrike vision.

Work hard play hard

At WaveStrike, we take an organized and streamlined approach to problems. We don’t do politics or egos. We focus on our customer; we work hard and hold ourselves to the highest performance standards. We stay agile and responsive, ensuring that nothing stops us from moving forward and delivering results.
We value our downtime and offer a very generous paid time off benefit. We believe that coming together as a company is important for team building and facilitates improved collaboration while on the job. Open communication between the leadership team and engineers is fostered by an open door policy as well as providing opportunities to come together through company lunches, employee events and voluntary participation in company sponsored events such as happy hours and golf tournaments.
Our engineers have a wide variety of interests outside of work. We enjoy game nights, softball, basketball, volleyball, golf, cycle, ski, curling and traveling. The journey here really is enjoyable, and we encourage everyone to bring your ideas and talents to make this the best place to work.
